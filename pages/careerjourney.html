Career Journey

Gaining both a BA in Public Relations and MA in Communication Management from the University of Alabama at Birmingham, my course studies have prepared me thoroughly for my career in communications. In addition, I was a student worker in the Media Relations department at the university for 3 years

My internship at Encompass Health(Formerly Healthouth) required me to update content on their network of sites using the SiteCore content management system and compile weekly reports of media mentions of the organization. I also gained experience crafting newsletters and researching and interviewing for stories.

My experience teaching communications and public speaking courses at Miles College gave me valuable skills in communication theory and organizing messaging. I also joined the grant writing workgroup that was instrumental in bringing an after school music program between the college and local area high schools.
